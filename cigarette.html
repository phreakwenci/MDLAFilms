2019 • Short • iPhone 7+ • 3 min

Cigarette

An experiment in restriction. Shot entirely on an iPhone 7+ using available light and architectural framing.

The Work

Director's Reflection

"Cigarette" was an exercise in finding the cinematic within the consumer-grade. By shooting on an iPhone 7+, I was forced to abandon the safety net of shallow depth-of-field.

I leaned into Suspense Geometry—using the vertical lines of the location to trap the character. The goal was to prove that atmosphere is a result of lighting and blocking, not the price of the sensor. High-contrast noir lighting turned a simple hallway into a psychological cage.
