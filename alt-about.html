MDLA is a technical film archive dedicated to the study of craft, constraint, and the transition from the Atlantic to the Pacific.

The MD > LA Logic

Maryland (MD) represents the Source. It is the place of origin, the foundation of a darker, atmospheric storytelling style rooted in regional reality.

Los Angeles (LA) represents the Instrument. It is the infrastructure, the tools, and the professional arena where the vision is executed.

The philosophy is simple: The vision (MD) must always remain greater than (>) the industry (LA).

Jan-David Soutar

I am a filmmaker and technical searcher. My work explores the darker possibilities of the human condition through a lens of structural discipline.

Influenced by the pacing of Stephen King and the technical constraints of early independent cinema, I believe that the director's job is not to find "the magic," but to build the Technical Architecture that allows magic to happen.

MDLA Films is not a portfolio. It is an open-source database of a director's education.
